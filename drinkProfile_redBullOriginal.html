Review

This drink is very popular due to the brand being so well known from the many events Red Bull sponsor including Formula 1, soap box challenge, Red Bull cliff diving and a whole host of others. This means that the price per ml is quite high for only a small taste improvement in my opinion.
Taste wise it is very sugary; the taste can be compared to sweet tarts due to the content of syrup and caramel. It is also worth mentioning that the branding on the can is very slick and the logo is very noticeable making it aesthetically pleasing is some regard.
My overall rating is only a 4/5 as the drink taste and can design are strong features of the product; the reason I didn’t rate it 5/5 is because on top of the high price per ml, there isn’t much availability of larger can sizes with cheaper price per ml (at least that is the case in the UK).
