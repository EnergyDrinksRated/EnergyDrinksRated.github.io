My Bio

When given the project at college to create a website I was very excited to learn how it was done and how to make my mark on the internet. The idea for the website came from an activity in which me and my friend have to try every new energy drink we find out of pure curiosity. Energy Drinks Rated was born from this to inform people about what energy drinks are available along with health benefits and drawbacks, nutritional advice, and a much-needed description for every ingredient.
Energy Drinks Rated is my first website which I started creating while learning web design and in that time, I have advanced my skills a long way. I am a 19-year-old computing student at college moving to university in September (2018) to continue learning about computers. Until then I am continuing to advance my web design skills further and am currently involved in 2 other web design projects for different businesses.
As of February 2018, Energy Drinks Rated will remain in its current state while I work on these other projects; however, more drinks will be added this year.
